
===============================

List of Reviews and lecture notes and leglly-online-avaiable
texts.  

First list is reviews/notes on all of machine learning or deep
learning.  Later some reviews on more specialized subtopics.  

===============================


* Review on deep learning in nature: 

https://www.nature.com/articles/nature14539


* Mehta et al's review on machine learning:

https://arxiv.org/abs/1803.08823


* Online book by Nielsen: 

http://neuralnetworksanddeeplearning.com/


* Online book by Goodfellow, Bengio, Courville: 

http://www.deeplearningbook.org/


* Course notes by Karpathy, Stanford CS231: 

http://cs231n.github.io/


* 2014 book by Shai Shalev-Shwartz and Shai Ben-David, online: 

https://www.cs.huji.ac.il/~shais/UnderstandingMachineLearning/copy.html


* Carleo et al's review "Machine Learning and the Physical Sciences": 

https://arxiv.org/abs/1903.10563


* Fan et al: "A Selective Overview of Deep Learning":

https://arxiv.org/abs/1904.05526


* 1996 Book by Raul Rojas, more theoretical: 

https://page.mi.fu-berlin.de/rojas/neural/


* Review by A.Jung on Machine Learning (not focused on deep NNs)

https://arxiv.org/abs/1805.05052


* O.Simeone: "A Brief Introduction to Machine Learning for Engineers",
more like a long review:

https://arxiv.org/abs/1709.02840
